PROOF SKETCH:

4. Apply the lemma that the cardinality of the union is at most the sum of the cardinalities of the individual sets.

theorem union_of_finite_subsets {s t : Set ℕ} (hs : s.Finite) (ht : t.Finite) : (s ∪ t).Finite :=
