2. **Time complexity of h(x) = f(g(x))**: The time to compute h(x) is the sum of the time to compute g(x) and the time to compute f(g(x)). 
3. **Polynomial bound on h**: Let p_h(n) = p_g(n) + p_f(p_g(n)). Since the composition of polynomials is a polynomial, and the sum of polynomials is a polynomial, p_h(n) is a polynomial in n. Thus, h is polynomial-time computable.
