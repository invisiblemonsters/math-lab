3. **Halting Guarantee**: Since both L₁ and L₂ are decidable, their respective Turing machines halt on any input. Thus, M will halt as soon as either simulation accepts, or both simulations reject.
4. **Correctness Verification**: Verify that M accepts w if and only if w ∈ L₁ or w ∈ L₂, ensuring the correctness of the union language's decidability.
